# Hello AI Lab Employee Portrait System v1

## Project
AI Employee Portrait System

## Status
- Version: v1.0
- Owner: Noah
- Purpose: Create a brand-consistent official portrait system for all Hello AI Lab employees

## Design Direction
The portrait system should feel like a premium creative company in the future: cinematic, minimal, real, and intelligent.

## Common Rules
- Realistic cinematic concept art quality
- Square composition
- Chest-up portrait
- Background transparent or clean neutral background
- Unified lighting direction
- Brand colors reflected through lighting, clothing, and accents
- Suitable for dashboard, employee card, and profile use

## Portrait Design Framework
Each portrait should follow these principles:
- The subject should look intelligent and calm
- The expression should feel composed rather than theatrical
- The clothing should be modern, premium, and understated
- The atmosphere should feel like a high-end creative company, not anime or stylized fantasy
- Use subtle cyan accents to connect with the brand system
