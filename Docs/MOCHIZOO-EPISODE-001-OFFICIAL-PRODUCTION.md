## ■ LUNA（音声班）- Audio Production Department

### Responsibilities
- Create child-friendly AI voice narration (4-5 seconds)
- Maintain consistent voice quality and tone across episodes
- Coordinate timing with video (narration plays during 3-10 second discovery phase)
- Ensure educational content is clear and simple English

### Episode001 Narration Script

**Original Text:**
```
Did you know baby pandas stay with their mom for almost two years?
```

### Production Specifications
- **Voice Type**: Female
- **Tone**: Warm, gentle, nurturing
- **Pace**: Slow and clear (easy for children to understand)
- **Language**: English
- **Duration**: 4-5 seconds
- **Timing**: Start at 3 seconds, end at 8-9 seconds (leaves 5-6 seconds silent at end)
- **Audio Format**: MP3/WAV, 44.1kHz or higher, mono or stereo
- **Quality**: Clear, no background noise, professional voice talent

### Voice Style Guide
```
Age Appeal:      2-8 years + parents
Emotion:         Educational + playful
Energy Level:    Calm and inviting (not energetic)
Pacing:          Deliberate, allow time for comprehension
Inflection:      Natural curiosity (emphasize "Did you know...?")
Clarity:         Enunciate clearly, simple English vocabulary
```

### Narration Timing
```
00:00 - 03:00   [Silent - Opening sequence]
03:00 - 08:00   [Narration plays - Panda discovering bamboo]
08:00 - 15:00   [Silent - Goodbye sequence]
```
