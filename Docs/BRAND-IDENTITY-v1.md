# Hello AI Lab Brand Identity v1

## Brand Concept
Hello AI Lab is not a chatbot tool company.
It is a company where AI employees work, collaborate, and grow.

The visual language should feel:
- simple
- premium
- futuristic
- calm
- cinematic
- precise

## Design Direction
Inspired by:
- Apple
- Linear
- Notion
- SF film control rooms

## Logo Direction
- Minimal and timeless
- Future-oriented
- Calm and intelligent
- Suitable for both product and internal systems

## Core Brand Colors
- Main Color: Cyan (#00BFFF)
- Sub Color: Deep Navy (#0F1B2B)
- Accent Color: Soft Silver (#DDE6F0)
- Highlight Color: Electric Blue (#2F6EFF)

## Typography Direction
- Clean sans-serif
- Modern and minimal
- High readability in internal systems

## Visual Rules
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep UI restrained and premium
- Avoid clutter
- Let color highlight only important states
- Use subtle motion and calm transitions

## Employee Visual Direction
- Each employee has a consistent visual language
- The design should feel professional and cinematic
- Characters should reflect personality but stay cohesive
