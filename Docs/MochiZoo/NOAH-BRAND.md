# Noah Brand Report: MochiZoo

## Branding Objective
Build a visual identity that feels warm, intelligent, premium, and child-safe while staying consistent with the Hello AI Lab brand system.

## Brand Direction
MochiZoo should feel like:
- gentle and trustworthy
- colorful but refined
- educational without feeling stiff
- fun without feeling chaotic

## Logo Direction
- Soft rounded shapes
- Friendly animal-inspired silhouette
- Calm, minimal, readable at small sizes
- A simple mark that can work across Shorts thumbnails and merchandise

## Character Design Direction
- Rounded shapes and expressive eyes
- Friendly proportions without looking overly toy-like
- Distinct personalities tied to each animal
- Clean silhouette for thumbnail recognition

## Brand Color System
- Primary: warm cyan and soft cream
- Accent: coral, soft lavender, or muted yellow for personality
- Neutral: warm white, soft gray, deep navy

## Font Direction
- Friendly but modern sans-serif
- Highly legible for children
- Clean for title cards and subtitles
- Should feel premium and approachable

## Worldview
MochiZoo is a gentle world where animals are characters, learning is soft, and every short feels like a small moment of delight.

## Brand Guidelines
- Keep visual density low.
- Use one clear emotional cue per frame.
- Make the animal the visual hero.
- Maintain a consistent and calm premium tone.

## Brand Book Update
The brand book should include:
- logo direction
- character language
- color palette
- typography
- thumbnail rules
- title rules
- brand voice
- visual system for future merchandise
