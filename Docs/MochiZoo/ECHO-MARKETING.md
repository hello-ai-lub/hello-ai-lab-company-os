# Echo Marketing Report: MochiZoo

## Objective
Build a cross-platform content engine that grows MochiZoo beyond YouTube Shorts.

## Recommended Platform Strategy
### YouTube Shorts
- Core growth engine
- Primary discovery channel
- Consistent publishing cadence

### Instagram Reels
- Share the best-performing Shorts with a slightly more polished aesthetic
- Use carousel posts for educational facts and character reveals

### TikTok
- Short, punchy, emotionally strong clips
- Use native-style pacing with strong hook and payoff

### X
- Share character moments, behind-the-scenes, and short educational posts
- Use quick commentary, milestone updates, and community interaction

## Title Style
- curiosity-led
- simple and direct
- child-friendly
- emotional or surprising

## Posting Time
Use test windows such as:
- early morning
- after school
- early evening

## Growth Tactics
- Cross-post top-performing Shorts
- Repackage one long concept into multiple short variations
- Use series branding to encourage repeat viewing
- Encourage parents to share content with children

## Fan Conversion Strategy
- Create character-based recurring content
- Encourage comments with simple prompts
- Build community around a few recognizable animals
- Reward repeat viewers with consistent series and recognizable formats
