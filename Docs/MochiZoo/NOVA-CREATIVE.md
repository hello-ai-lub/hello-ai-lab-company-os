# Nova Creative Report: MochiZoo

## Creative Objective
Design a production system that can generate a high volume of Shorts without sacrificing clarity, charm, or quality.

## Recommended Shorts Structure
### Hook (0–3 sec)
Use a clear visual surprise or emotional trigger.
Examples:
- A baby animal doing something unexpected
- A tiny animal with a huge reaction
- An animal in a cozy or funny situation

### Middle (3–10 sec)
Deliver one simple fact or mini-story.

### Ending (10–15 sec)
End with a satisfying emotional payoff or a gentle reveal.

## Repeatable Creative Formats
1. Guess the animal
2. Tiny animal skill
3. Animal home tour
4. Animal sound challenge
5. Animal friendship story
6. Sleepy animal moment

## 100-Video Creative Map
