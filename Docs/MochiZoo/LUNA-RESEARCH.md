# Luna Research Report: MochiZoo

## Research Objective
Find the most valuable insights that can increase growth, retention, and future brand strength.

## High-Value Research Findings
### 1. Popular animal topics
- baby animals
- tiny animals
- cute animal behavior
- animal homes
- unusual animal skills
- animal sleep habits

### 2. Educational categories that work well
- animal facts
- animal habitats
- animal sounds
- animal size comparisons
- animal behavior
- animal care and kindness

### 3. What tends to perform in global short-form content
- simple emotional framing
- one clear takeaway
- strong visual contrast
- fast pacing
- familiar animals that feel fresh

### 4. Child-friendly video patterns that work
- simple narration
- repeated visual rhythm
- gentle sound design
- warm pacing
- optimistic ending

### 5. New ideas to test
- “Animal of the day” series
- “Tiny animal superpower” series
- “Animal home tour” series
- “Animal sleep story” series
- “Guess the animal” challenge series

## Recommended Research Priority
Focus on topics that combine:
- cuteness
- basic learning
- repeatability
- recognizability

## Research Note
The strongest opportunity is not to find the rarest animals, but to find the most lovable, repeatable, and teachable animal moments.
